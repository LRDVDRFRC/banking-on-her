Use Case

Bank in NL

Client

Mid-size Dutch bank with 200k+ retail clients. Wealth management arm underserving women clients who now control 52% of inherited assets.

Problem

Products designed for male clients. Female client attrition at 3x industry average after inheritance events. Estimated annual revenue loss: EUR 12M.

IBC Intervention

Readiness Scan (Day 1) revealed D-rating across propositions and marcom. Advisory trajectory redesigned 3 key products. Academy certified 24 internal practitioners.

Result

Female client retention improved 41%. New wealth product generated EUR 8.2M in first year. Bank became IBC Certified Gender Lab partner.
